    About Us

    Launched in the middle of 2021, Tale.Traders is a new and fast-growing online bookstore, aiming to fulfill all your fictional dreams. Made by a team of avid readers who understand the urge of desiring a book but not having a physical bookstore nearby, we're here to make sure that book you're longing for gets to you as fast as it can. We have a large collection of books in all genre, from the classics to the bestsellers fresh from printing, and at a fair pricing. Feel free to communicate with us through our email or listed social media if you have any queries, questions or complaints. We're always keeping an ear out for ways to improve and satisfy our customer base.
